Hi, I'm Aroofa Mohammad

Infrastructure Engineer

I build and support reliable technology solutions through infrastructure, networking, cloud technologies, and automation. I enjoy solving complex problems and continuously expanding my technical skills.

My Story

I was born and raised in Austin, Texas, where my journey into technology started in 6th grade when I joined my school's robotics team. I didn't know it at the time, but building robots would eventually turn into a career of writing code, solving problems, and occasionally asking "why is this not working?" for way longer than I want to admit.

I continued developing my skills at The University of Texas at Dallas, where I earned my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. During my time at UTD, I strengthened my technical abilities while learning the importance of collaboration, teamwork, and building solutions with others.

After graduating, I returned to Austin and began my professional career at Eagle Eye Networks Inc. There, I gained hands-on experience working with computer networks, Linux systems, troubleshooting, and enterprise technology solutions. This opportunity allowed me to apply what I learned in school to real-world infrastructure and networking challenges.

Now I'm continuing to grow as an Infrastructure Engineer, exploring cloud technologies, automation, and new challenges. And yes, I still believe Austin has the best food, best music, and the best excuse for being late: "I got stuck on I-35."
